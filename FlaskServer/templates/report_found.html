Tips for Reporting Found Items
  • Include essential details but withhold some specific information
  • Take a clear photo, but consider blurring identifying marks
  • Be specific about where and when you found the item
  • Check if the item has contact information on it
  • Consider turning the item in to a local lost and found office
What Happens Next?

After submitting your report, the found item will be visible to everyone on RecoVer. If someone claims to be the owner, we'll help connect you with them.

Before handing over the item, ask the person claiming it to describe specific details that only the true owner would know.
